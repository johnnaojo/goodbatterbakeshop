About the bakery

Beautifully designed treats that still taste like the main event.

Johnna, Owner and Baker

Since 2020, Good Batter Bakeshop has been serving up gorgeous and delicious baked treats; cakes, cupcakes, cookies, cake pops, and other amazing desserts. Whether its beautifully designed cakes and cupcakes, or themed realistic treats, Johnna's creations are designed to wow you! But its not just about looks, the cakes taste great! From delicious red velvet, sublime vanilla, rich chocolate, fruity strawberry, to buttery and nutty butter pecan, you'll love the flavor of your cake.
